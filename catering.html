Chicken Briyani

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and flavorful saffron rice. For my Biryani, I simplify the order of operations, while retaining the traditional layered approach to assembling it.

Mutton Briyani

Mutton biryani is a popular Indian rice dish made using long grain basmati rice layered with a spicy mutton (goat meat) layer. You can also alternatively make it with lamb. It is flavorful, delicious, and will make you wipe your plates clean with its incredible taste and beautiful aroma!

Our services


Large event plans

Every big event you cater takes meticulous planning to run smoothly and successfully. A huge part of your catering preparation should be visually laying out the event space, so you, your staff, and your client know exactly where to go and where all the pieces will be. Still, the little details can change based on your event and venue, and each one of those details needs should be mapped out in your plan.


Medium event plans

In today’s time, catering service has become an essential part of any business event like dealers meeting, award functions, team gathering and more. Such events normally involve long hours business discussions, corporate talks, business plans etc. Delicious food with wonderful service is necessary to feel energize after long duration events, otherwise it might affect other vital factors such as productivity and commitment.


Small event plans

When it comes to organize a small party event, we aren’t short of ideas. We’re not only famed for our culinary expertise but we’re also event management specialists and can take care of everything you need for your small party event.
